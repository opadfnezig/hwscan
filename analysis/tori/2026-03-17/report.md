## Limitations

- **No sold vs expired distinction** — tori.fi scraper currently treats "Myyty" (sold) badge identically to 404/removal. All show as "deleted".
- **Only 14 days of data** — deletion rates for recent days are artificially low (items haven't had time to sell/expire yet)
- **No seller data** — tori.fi doesn't expose seller name/ID in server-rendered HTML
- **No posted_at** — can't calculate time-to-sale or listing age
